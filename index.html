Rules & eligibility

The fine print

Who qualifies +

All three conditions must be met:

  • Spend on eSIM data or utility bills (electricity, cable TV, internet, water) via TradeCard — gift card trades and top-ups excluded.
  • Reach at least ₦1,000 cumulative eligible spend in the campaign window.
  • Be following TradeCard on Instagram at the time of post-campaign verification.
How ranking works +
  • Ranked purely by total cumulative eligible spend within the campaign window.
  • Only eSIM and utility bill transactions count.
  • Spend counts up to 23:59 WAT on 19 July 2026.
  • Ties broken by who reached the total first.
  • Weekly leaderboard is a live snapshot and does not guarantee a reward.

Disqualification & terms +
  • Spend under ₦1,000, or not following on Instagram at verification.
  • Fraudulent, reversed, or disputed transactions.
  • Any manipulation or gaming of the ranking system.
  • If a Top 10 user is disqualified, the next eligible spender moves up.
  • Rewards are direct cash to the TradeCard wallet. Rankings are unaffected by Instagram following. TradeCard is not affiliated with or endorsed by FIFA.
